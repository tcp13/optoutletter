Sending Instructions

Print the opt out letter and mail it to whoever won't leave you alone. Address the envelope to the company's name, with "ATTN: Privacy Compliance Officer" on the second line, followed by the corporate mailing address (which can typically be found by searching online). If they've sent you physical mail, you can respond to the provided return address or even send it through any prepaid return envelopes.

Remember, just because you've sent an opt out letter doesn't guarantee the company will actually comply with your request! Visit the FAQ page to learn more about the need for comprehensive federal privacy regulations.
